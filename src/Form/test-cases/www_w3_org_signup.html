Account request

We welcome new participants to the W3C community.

Please complete this form to request a W3C Account. Accounts are not required for access to most of the W3C site, which is public. Please create an account only if you are participating in a group at W3C. We do not currently publish profile pages for people with W3C Accounts.

If you already have a W3C Account and you don't remember your credentials, you can use the W3C Account recovery page.

Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.

Passwords our algorithm deems weak will be rejected
Passwords must be at least 8 characters long
Passwords should mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers and special characters
