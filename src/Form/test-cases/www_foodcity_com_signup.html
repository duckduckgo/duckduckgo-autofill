    Shopping Lists Stay organized with access to all of your shopping lists. You can also use your list(s) to locate items by their aisle locations in the store.

    Digital Offers With Digital Coupons, we are able to save you time and money by clipping coupons directly to your ValuCard to be redeemed instantly at checkout. Coupon screen shows available and loaded savings and can be sorted by your preference.

    Reserve Pickup Time Placing an order for curbside pickup is easy with our Timeslot feature. Simply select your desired pickup time and it will be reserved for you.

    Meal Planner You can group recipes into meal plans and easily add all the ingredients to your cart or lists!

    Recipes Recipes are integrated throughout the app, from cart to lists. Under Recipes, you can search for any recipe and even get suggestions based on what you like.

    Account Management Access all of your account information, check Fuel Bucks, request a new ValuCard, check upcoming orders and manage your saved credit cards all in our new secure account management dashboard.
