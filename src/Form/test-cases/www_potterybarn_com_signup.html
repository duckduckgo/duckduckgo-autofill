FAQs

How do I earn Key Rewards?

Cardmembers earn 5% back in Gold rewards1 on qualified purchases with their Key Rewards credit card across our brands (the highest level of rewards and benefits). Members can join for free and earn 2% back in Silver rewards1 on qualified purchases across our brands.

Can I earn Gold and Silver rewards on the same purchase?

You will earn either 5% back in Gold rewards as a cardmember1 or 2% back in Silver rewards as a member1 on a transaction, not both.

When will purchases be added to my account?

Your account balance will be updated 48 hours after purchase or shipment.

When will I receive my Key Rewards?

30 days after earning $10 or more in Silver rewards, or $10 or more in Gold rewards, a reward code will be sent to your inbox. It will also be available on your Key Rewards dashboard on our brand websites.

Where can I use my Key Rewards Visa Card?

The Pottery Barn Key Rewards Visa can be used across our collection of brands and anywhere else that Visa is accepted. The Pottery Barn Key Rewards Card can only be used across our collection of brands.
